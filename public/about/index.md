# About BrewKit Lab

Hey, we're BrewKit Lab — a small shop focused on helping beginners get into homebrewing without the intimidation.

## Who We Are

We started BrewKit Lab because we believe everyone deserves to try making their own beer, cider, or ale — and it shouldn't require a degree in fermentation science to get started.

## What We Sell

- 1-Gallon Beginner Brewing Kits
- 5-Gallon Beginner Brewing Kits

## Our Philosophy

- No experience required
- Quality beginner equipment at fair prices
- Ships everywhere
